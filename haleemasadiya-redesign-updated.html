Our Story

About Haleema Sadiya Orphanage

Operating in Kadapa, Andhra Pradesh, the Haleema Sadiya Muslim Girls' Orphanage opened its doors in 2005 with a humble beginning, caring for a few orphan and destitute girls.

Today, it cares for around 100 orphan girls and has become a model institution. It works under the registered name of Al-Ehsan Muslim Orphanage Girls Educational & Welfare Society, Kadapa, under the supervision of Janab Mufti Raheemuallah Khan.

Our Courses & Curriculum

The Madrasa-e-Haleema Sadiya Niswan runs from 11th June to 30th April each academic year, offering two comprehensive Islamic programs alongside modern education.

📿
3 Year Program
Hafiza Course

Complete memorization of the Holy Quran. Students can enroll after completing 8th or 10th Standard. This prestigious program develops a deep spiritual connection and memory discipline.

  • Quran Memorization
  • Tajweed
  • Islamic Values
🎓
7 Year Program
Aalima Course

A comprehensive Islamic scholars program covering theology, jurisprudence, Arabic grammar and more. Paired with modern subjects and vocational training.

  • Elm e Tajveed
  • Fiqah
  • Arabic Grammar
  • Elm e Hadeeth
  • Tafseer
  • Seerah
💡
Ongoing Skills
Self-Employment Training

Practical vocational skills integrated into the curriculum to empower girls to become financially independent after graduation.

  • Tailoring
  • Wire Bag Weaving
  • Wool Curtains
  • Painting
  • Computer Training
